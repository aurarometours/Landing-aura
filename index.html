Tour Details

The price includes an elegant golf cart (Alba or Melex for larger groups), a professional driver, fresh water and wipes on board.

Tours normally last around 2.5 hours with multiple stops of about 10–15 minutes.

Start directly from the hotel or a meeting point in the historic centre.

Routes and duration can be customized depending on guest preferences.

Museum or monument entrance tickets are not included.

Payment methods: cash, credit card (+10%), PayPal, Revolut or instant bank transfer.
