Tour Details

The price includes the rental of an elegant golf cart (Alba or Melex for larger groups), a professional driver, fresh water and wipes on board.

Tours normally last about 2.5 hours and include multiple stops of around 10–15 minutes each.

It is possible to start directly from the hotel or another meeting point in the city centre.

Routes and duration can be customized according to guest preferences.

Museum or monument entrance tickets are not included.

Payment can be made in cash, credit card (+10%), PayPal, Revolut or instant bank transfer.
